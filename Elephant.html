Elephants

The elephant's gestation is 22 months which is longer than any land animal in the world.

  • Species: African and Asian
  • Diet: Herbivorious
  • Habitat: Savannahs, forests, deserts and marshes
  • Life Expectancy: up to 70 years in the wild

Elephants are the largest existing land animals and communicate by touch, sight, smell and sound. They use infrasound and seismic communication over long distances.Males (Bulls)leave their family groups when they reach puberty and may live alone or with other males
